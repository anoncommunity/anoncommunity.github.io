WHAT IS A NODE?

A full node is a program that validates transactions. They also help the network by accepting transactions from other full nodes, validating those transactions and then relaying them onward (to other nodes).

WHY ARE THEY NEEDED?

Without nodes, there will be nothing to connect to – wallets wouldn’t be able to connect, nodes wouldn’t be able to connect to other nodes – in short, there would be no ZClassic.
