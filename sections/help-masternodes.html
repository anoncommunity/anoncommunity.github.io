⚠️ Community engagement - Mining ⛏

Fellow community members, the team is currently testing an official miningpool for ANON and working to ensure that everything operates smoothly. To accumulate the substantial hashing power in the pool required to find blocks, we would encourage you to participate in mining ANON. Here is a short briefing on how you can contribute:

I have a mining-rig and certain hashpower!

Great, you are the right one we are looking for. If you want to allocate some of your hashing power to the pool, we would be pleased to maintain the strength of the nethash. You might prefer your own miner or software - just scroll down to find the connection data.

Not a miner? You still can help out!

If you have a high performing graphics card with at least 4GB vRAM you can use a miningsoftware to feed the pool with some hashing power. This is a pre-zipped EWFB 0.5 miner with the ANON batchfile, just unzip it and adjust the config with rightclick and edit - You will see the following: miner --algo 144_5 --pers AnonyPoW --server xeropool.com --port 3333 --user your_wallet_address.your_name --pass anon Change "your_wallet_address" to you wallet address beginning with "An" and additional change "your_name" to your nickname. Save the file and double click the batchfile "start_anon". https://drive.google.com/file/d/1agHozeSczhoIzrhNl9vyUYWmFZn-QnR9/view?usp=sharing

Found a block but didn't receive a payment?

Since this is a testing phase we do not want any skeptical prejudices - please be assured: If you have found a block and did NOT get a reward, we will credit you manually! If this happens, simply contact @Xarxero with the blockhash data. Don't forget, it will ALWAYS take 100 Blocks to mature before being sent to your address.
